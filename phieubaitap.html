Reported Speech Exercises

Bài 1: Chuyển câu trực tiếp sang câu gián tiếp

1. "I will go to the market tomorrow," he said. → He said
2. "She is studying for her exam," Tom said. → Tom said
3. "We have finished our homework," they said. → They said
4. "I can help you with your project," Peter told me. → Peter told me
5. "It's raining heavily outside," Jane said. → Jane said

Bài 3: Chọn đáp án đúng

1. He said, "I am going to the party tonight." → He said that
a) I am going to the party tonight
b) he was going to the party tonight
c) he is going to the party tonight
2. "We have never been to Paris," they said. → They said that
a) they have never been to Paris
b) they had never been to Paris
c) they were never in Paris
3. "I will call you later," she said. → She said that
a) she will call me later
b) she would call me later
c) she has called me later
4. "I am eating lunch," John said. → John said that
a) he is eating lunch
b) he was eating lunch
c) he has eaten lunch
5. "He can swim very fast," she said. → She said that
a) he can swim very fast
b) he could swim very fast
c) he is swimming very fast

Bài 6: Chọn đáp án đúng

1. "Can you help me with this project?" he asked. → He asked if
a) I can help him with this project
b) I could help him with this project
c) I am helping him with this project
2. "When does your flight leave?" she asked me. → She asked me
a) when my flight leaves
b) when my flight left
c) when my flight would leave
3. "Where are you going for your holiday?" they asked. → They asked
a) where I was going for my holiday
b) where I am going for my holiday
c) where I went for my holiday
4. "Have you finished your homework?" she asked. → She asked
a) if I had finished my homework
b) if I have finished my homework
c) if I finish my homework
5. "Will you come to the party?" she asked. → She asked
a) if I would come to the party
b) if I will come to the party
c) if I am coming to the party
